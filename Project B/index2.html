SAVE THE SEA

INSTRUCTION Player1 CLICK TO RELEASE WAIT TILL IT IS BACK Player2 MOVE THE DEAD FISH
DESCRIPTION Plastic waste has polluted the ocean. You and another player will be attempting to save the ocean in this game. Only bottles can be caught on the boat's hook; fish are not harmed in any way. To avoid being directly hung on the boat, a magnetic system was used. If the small fish consumes the bottles twice, it will perish. Make every effort to protect the ocean.
